# Ota2015 - Positive regulation of Rho GTPase activity by RhoGDIs as a result of their direct interaction with GAPs (GDI integrated)

This is a ordinary differential equation mathematical model describing the Rho GTPase cycle in which Rho GDP-dissociation inhibitors (RhoGDIs) inhibit the regulatory activities of guanine nucleotide e. It can be used to explore systemsbiology ota2015 positive regulation of rho dynamics and compare simulation behavior across conditions.
