# Ma2005-digital response of p53 to DNA damage-ATM Activation Module

Recent observations show that the single-cell response of p53 to ionizing radiation (IR) is “digital” in that it is the number of oscillations rather than the amplitude of p53 that shows dependence on. It can be used to explore systemsbiology ma2005 digital response of p53 dynamics and compare simulation behavior across conditions.
