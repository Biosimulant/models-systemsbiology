# Scheidel2016 - In Silico Knockout Studies of Xenophagy Capturing of Salmonella

Xenophagy, also known as antibacterial autophagy, is a process of capturing and eliminating cytosolic pathogens, like Salmonella. Salmonella is the best-studied model organism for xenophagy.
