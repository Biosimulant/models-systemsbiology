# Patel2023 - miRNA-199a/b-5p regionation of chondrogenesis - enchanced

From a previous microarray study we developed a small chondrogenesis model. We performed qPCR and measured how knockdown of miR-199a-5p or miR-199b-5p could modulate chondrogenesis.
