# Bravo2012 - Modelling blood coagulation factor Va inactivation by APC

Mathematical model of blood coagulation factor Va and Va fragment inactivation by APC, reactions with Xa and prothrombinase-prothrombin complex formation. It can be used to explore systemsbiology bravo2012 modelling blood coagulation factor dynamics and compare simulation behavior across conditions.
